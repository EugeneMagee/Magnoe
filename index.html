Data Deletion Request

If you would like to request deletion of your personal data associated with an app or service provided by Magnoe, please follow the steps below:

  1. Send an email to service@magnoe.co.uk
  2. Include the subject line: Data Deletion Request
  3. In the email body, provide the following details:
    • Your full name
    • Your username or email used in the app
    • The name of the app or service you're referencing

Once we receive your request, we will verify your identity and delete your data from our systems within 7 business days. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

For any other questions, feel free to contact us at service@magnoe.co.uk.
